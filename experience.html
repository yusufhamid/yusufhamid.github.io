Summer language/leadership camp:

During my junior year of high school 2013, I attended OneWorld Now! language and leadership camp for three weeksat Seattle Pacific University.
The OneWorld Now! language program focuses on a student-centered learning and interactive approach to critical language learning. OWN offers Modern Standard Arabic classes or Mandarin Chinese classes, and I was in Modern Standard Arabic class.
The OneWorld Now! leadership program is foundation for creating the next generation of global leaders concerned with social justice and equality. The OWN leadership program guides students through the following phases: discovery, vision, action, and reflection.


Muslim Youth Leadership Program
CAIR-WA

In my sophomore year of high school 2012, I attended Muslim Youth Leadership Program.
MYLP is an intense, exciting four day event in Seattle designed to introduce American Muslim youth to powerful careers where professionals have opportunities to shape public opinion and public policy as a course of their work.
MYLP helps improve networking skills and Executive Office Visits four days of back-to-back meetings with local and national political leaders, high-powered executives of local TV and radio stations, law firms, public relations firms, campaign management firms, and large philanthropic foundations at their own Seattle offices.
As participant, it allowed me to open up my mind and consider different ways that we as young adults could give back to our communities.

Seattle African Cup - Organizer
African Sports Federation

Seattle African Cup is to build a better community by promoting sports, Cultural and educational programs. Our goals include providing positive role models for the youth, promoting goodwill among African communities in Seattle, WA and creating a bridge where the diversified communities in Seattle, can interact in a mutually beneficial manner. Most of all, to hold an annual soccer tournament that will provide a time of quality soccer, showcasing a variety of talent from many ethnic backgrounds and ages from Seattle and around the world. To provide player development; encourage the participation of women and children; and to organize functions throughout the year that will provide an excellent opportunity for cultural events.
